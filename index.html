About me

I am currently a Computer Engineer at Turkiye Ministry of Health.

I have worked as software project manager, pl/sql software developer, decision support system specialist, and use Oracle BI, other business intelligence tools.

I obtained my PhD thesis in Computer Science in Ankara Yildirim Beyazit University Graduate School of Natural Sciences. I have finished my PhD within 5 years. My PhD topic was `Cerebrospinal Fluid Flow Segmentation And Feature Extraction Of Phase Contrast Mri Using Deep Learning Technique': I proposed a new automatic CSF lumen segmentation pipeline.
